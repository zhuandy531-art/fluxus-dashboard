# High ADR% and Momentum Screeners

> [!summary]
> Jeff runs three related momentum-focused screeners: the High ADR% Hottest Stock screener (TradingView + Finviz), the KC-inspired Extended Base screener (Finviz), and the Strongest Mover screener across four timeframes (1W/1M/3M/6M). Together these capture both high-volatility leaders and stocks building prolonged bases before explosive moves.

## Core Concept

High ADR% (Average Daily Range percentage) is central to Jeff's trading philosophy. Securities with higher ADR% offer greater potential return per unit of capital deployed. These screeners identify stocks with outsized daily ranges, strong multi-timeframe momentum, or prolonged consolidation patterns that precede large moves.

The Qullamaggie-style variant specifically targets the type of setups that produced historic moves like TASR in 2003-2004 -- low float, high ADR%, high relative volume leaders.

> "The best % performers almost always have these three ingredients." (Low market cap, low float %, high short float)

## Rules / Details

### High ADR% Hottest Stock Screener

**TradingView Version**: Screens for stocks with elevated ADR%, filtering for the "hottest" names by combining daily range with volume and price action criteria.

**Finviz Version (2025)**: Updated Finviz filters calibrated for the 2025 market environment.

**Finviz Qullamaggie Style (2003-2004 TASR)**: A specialized variant inspired by Qullamaggie's approach, targeting the characteristics found in the best historical percentage performers. Jeff pulled all top 100% performers from 2018 to 2024 for study, and this screener reflects those patterns.

### KC-Inspired Extended Base Screener

Inspired by Jeff's 2024 trade on KC that returned +180%, this screener targets extended bases and prolonged consolidations such as Cup & Handle patterns.

> "Does the stock want to go higher?"

This question is the guiding filter for this scan. The screener looks for stocks that have consolidated for extended periods, contracting in volatility (VCP-like behavior), and appear poised for a breakout. The Finviz 2025 version captures these prolonged consolidation patterns.

### Strongest Mover Screener (1W / 1M / 3M / 6M)

This screener ranks stocks by performance across four lookback periods:

- **1-Week**: Captures very recent momentum shifts
- **1-Month**: Identifies emerging leaders
- **3-Month**: Confirms intermediate-term strength
- **6-Month**: Reveals durable leadership

Both TradingView and Finviz versions are available. The Finviz mobile web screener version is particularly useful for quick checks during work hours.

> "Perks of the Finviz mobile web screener: effortless to use at work or on breaks."

## How Jeff Applies It

These three screeners complement each other in Jeff's workflow. The High ADR% screen finds the most volatile actionable names. The KC-inspired screen catches slower-developing setups that may not yet appear on momentum screens but are coiling for a significant move. The Strongest Mover screen provides a multi-timeframe overlay to confirm which stocks are genuinely leading.

Results from all three are consolidated into purpose-specific watchlists during post-market analysis, then evaluated alongside CANSLIM and other screener results before stocks are promoted to the Focus List.
