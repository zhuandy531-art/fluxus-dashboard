# Free Tools

> [!summary]
> Jeff has compiled extensive lists of free productivity and trading tools across multiple years: Top 8 (2023), Top 9 (2024), and a massive 25 free tools list (2024). He also shares Twitter/X productivity hacks, FinanceCharts.com as a favorite free resource, and uses free tools for tasks like analyzing Druckenmiller's F-13 filings.

## Core Concept

Jeff consistently emphasizes that you do not need expensive tools or paid services to trade profitably. His entire workflow is built on free or low-cost resources — TradingView, Finviz, Twitter/X, and a collection of free websites that most traders have never heard of.

## How Jeff Applies It

Jeff's daily workflow integrates these free tools into a systematic process:

1. **Post-market**: Finviz screeners and TradingView screeners for watchlist generation
2. **Pre-market**: Twitter/X for sentiment and breaking news, TradingView for price alerts
3. **Live market**: TradingView for charting, RVOL monitoring, and execution
4. **Research**: FinanceCharts, F-13 analysis tools, and free data websites for deeper analysis

His Excel-based market diary [[sources/tweets/jfsrev-1659648518867554304]] and relative strength histogram [[sources/tweets/jfsrev-1806709652975141131]] are built entirely with free tools and publicly available data.

A free Google Sheet version of his RS histogram was also created by @RonakSh92987573 [[sources/tweets/jfsrev-1807966765223498058]].
