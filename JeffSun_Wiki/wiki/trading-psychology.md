# Trading Psychology

> [!summary]
> Chapter 14 of Jeff's Complete Guide contains his highest-impression personal tweets — raw reflections on grit, ego, money vs. life, the danger of chasing trades, the power of simplicity, and why trading is more like farming than gambling. These are organized here by theme with links to original posts.

## Core Concept

Jeff's psychological framework emerges not from a single lesson but from years of lived experience, losses, and hard-won perspective. Chapter 14 compiles his most resonant posts — each one a crystallized insight from the trenches.

## How Jeff Applies It

Jeff's 15-year trading journey (shared in 2024 [[sources/tweets/jfsrev-1803003192126677133]]) demonstrates these principles in practice. His tax bill in 2023 exceeded 100k USD despite no capital gains tax in Singapore. By June 2024, he attained his 12th property purchase, crossing 10 million USD in real estate [[sources/tweets/jfsrev-1803396643687166425]].

These results came not from a single breakthrough trade but from years of consistent application of the mindset principles above.
