# Trade Example: RDDT

> [!summary]
> RDDT (Reddit) trade from 5th June 2024 is Jeff's second documented example of the [[3-stop-strategy]] in action. As a recent IPO with social media thematic momentum, RDDT likely surfaced through Jeff's [[ipo-screener]] and demonstrates how his system handles high-ADR% IPO momentum names.

## Setup & Entry

RDDT is referenced in the context of the [[3-stop-strategy]] as another practical example:

> "Another example with RDDT from 5th June 2024"
> — [[sources/notion/complete-guide-snapshot|Complete Guide, Chapter 6]]

Reddit went public in March 2024 and would have appeared in Jeff's [[ipo-screener]] (Screener #5), which he runs on a weekly frequency using Finviz:

> "IPO screener (Screen Frequency: Weekly) - Finviz Version (only Finviz filtering parameter is capable to do this)"
> — [[sources/notion/complete-guide-snapshot|Complete Guide, Chapter 3]]

As a recent IPO, RDDT had the characteristics Jeff looks for:

- **No overhead resistance**: New IPOs that are building bases above their IPO price have no prior shareholders looking to sell at a loss, reducing supply overhead.
- **High ADR%**: As a newly public social media stock with significant retail interest, RDDT offered the high [[adr-percent]] that Jeff requires for meaningful R-multiples.
- **Thematic momentum**: Social media / user-generated-content as a theme was showing strength in 2024.

## Trade Management

Jeff uses the RDDT trade specifically to illustrate the [[3-stop-strategy]] execution mechanics. The trade from 5th June 2024 serves as a complement to the [[ita-trade|ITA trade example]] -- showing the same 3-stop framework applied to a different instrument type (high-growth IPO vs. sector ETF).

The 3-stop structure on RDDT would have:

1. Split the position into three equal portions (33% each)
2. Set initial stops to cap downside at approximately -0.67R
3. Managed exits systematically as price developed

Specific scaling and partial profit details are not documented beyond the 3-stop framework demonstration.

## Exit & Result

Specific profit/loss figures for the RDDT trade are not detailed in the source material. The trade is primarily documented as a teaching example for the [[3-stop-strategy]], paired with the [[ita-trade|ITA trade]] to show the strategy's application across different instrument types and market conditions.

## Key Lessons

1. **IPO screener surfaces opportunities**: Jeff's weekly IPO screening cadence identified RDDT as a potential focus list candidate. The screener's weekly frequency (rather than daily) reflects that IPO setups develop on a longer timeline.
2. **3-stop strategy works across instrument types**: By pairing the RDDT example (high-growth IPO) with the ITA example (sector ETF), Jeff demonstrates that the [[3-stop-strategy]] is instrument-agnostic.
3. **Recent IPOs have structural advantages**: No overhead resistance from prior shareholders, fresh institutional interest, and often high ADR% -- all characteristics Jeff seeks.
4. **Timing within the IPO lifecycle**: The trade date (5th June 2024) was approximately 2.5 months after Reddit's March 2024 IPO, suggesting Jeff waited for the stock to build a base and show a VCP before entering, rather than chasing the initial IPO pop.
