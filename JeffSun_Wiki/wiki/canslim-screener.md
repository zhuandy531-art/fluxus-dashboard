# CANSLIM-Inspired Screener

> [!summary]
> Jeff Sun's CANSLIM-inspired calibrated screener is his most popular shared screen, accumulating over 1.9 million Twitter impressions. Available in TradingView and Finviz versions, it filters for stocks exhibiting the growth characteristics associated with the CANSLIM methodology. The 2025 Finviz version adds an institutional transaction filter.

## Core Concept

The CANSLIM methodology, originally developed by William O'Neil, identifies leading growth stocks through a combination of current earnings, annual earnings, new products/management, supply and demand, leader vs. laggard status, institutional sponsorship, and market direction. Jeff's screener is a calibrated adaptation of these principles, tuned for his swing trading style and applied across both TradingView and Finviz platforms.

This screener is the first in Jeff's battery of 14 post-market screeners and serves as a foundational scan for identifying quality growth candidates before further watchlist curation.

## Rules / Details

### TradingView Version
The TradingView version of the CANSLIM screener uses TradingView's built-in screener functionality with custom column configurations and filters. As of October 2025, the exact configuration is shareable via TradingView's Screen Sharing feature.

### Finviz Version (2025)
The 2025 Finviz iteration introduces a key enhancement: the **Institutional Transaction filter**. This addition layers institutional buying/selling activity on top of the fundamental and technical CANSLIM criteria, providing an additional signal of smart money accumulation or distribution.

> "Leveraging it now for the 'Institutional Transaction' filter."

### Finviz Mobile Web Screener
Jeff also uses Finviz's mobile web screener for quick CANSLIM-based checks during work hours or breaks. The mobile version provides the same filtering capabilities in a portable format.

> "Perks of the Finviz mobile web screener: effortless to use at work or on breaks."

## How Jeff Applies It

The CANSLIM screener runs daily as part of post-market analysis. Results are shifted into a dedicated watchlist, where they are then reviewed alongside results from the other 13 screeners. Stocks that pass the CANSLIM screen still require further evaluation through watchlist management and focus list criteria before becoming actionable.

The screener's popularity (1.9M+ Twitter impressions) reflects its utility as an accessible starting point for traders building a growth-focused watchlist. However, Jeff is clear that screening alone does not produce trades -- it produces candidates for deeper analysis.

The 2025 institutional transaction filter on Finviz adds a layer that aligns with Jeff's emphasis on volume confirmation and institutional sponsorship as prerequisites for sustainable price expansion.
