Intraday Change % Comparison: Strongest 1-Month RS Groups vs Weakest 1-Month RS Groups

Always prioritize relative strength first, setup second. Try working it top-down: from segement i.e. $IVE value vs $IVW growth, to sector i.e. $XLV vs $XLC, to industry groups. You’ll often find the stock setups within the strongest groups will not take out your LoD stops randomly/easily with price action resilience. You can even simplify it by trading the RS group ETFs directly:
$XAGUSD $XAUUSD $BOIL $LABU $IBB $LIT

If you are good at finding setup but not getting results, you need to continuously refine your process to reduce randomness and improve consistency in your monthly trading review. What may appear to be a “5-star” setup in your eyes might not be validated by the market — unless there’s clear accumulation from institutional players to begin with.
