## Tweet Content

Everyone knows +6% monthly compounded gain will give you a +100% annualized gain.

But very few dissect this 6% gain into what is required of your average trading proficiency. Let me give a quick but conservative case of 25% win rate month revolving controlled avg R loss at -0.7, and purely a 0.17% risk to equity of start month.

1. Risk: 0.17% risk to equity per trade (a single R loss is 0.17% of account equity)
2. Trade frequency: 64 trades, 16 win, 48 loss.
3. Win rate: 25% only
4. Avg R gain 4.3 multiples, Avg Holding Period For winning trade (<15 days)
5. Avg R loss -0.7 multiple, Avg Holding Period For Losing trade (<4 days)
6. Profit Factor: 6.14
7. Gain to Pain: 2.1

If you use this as a benchmark and strive to improve the numbers here, control what you can control specifically on your losses, you can hit +100% year even with a few controlled losing months.
