This letter from a fellow countryman trader who was once a prominent figure (ex-Salomon Brothers from Singapore) with 39 years of experience was initially circulated within the fund management community, but given its subsequent public dissemination in Reuters and Bloomberg, I find it valuable to share and emphasize certain key insights.

This is an undeniable reality in the financial market.
