## Tweet Content

By acknowledging that trading can result in losses, we approach each position with a different mindset and adapt our thinking that we may be wrong until proven otherwise. It is essential for the market to prove, and continue to prove its validity. 

Avoid trading under the misconception that everyone achieves success in trading. Instead, let our actions be guided by the goal of preserving our gains rather than solely focusing on protecting against losses. Risk management involves a deeper, and more proactive approach than merely setting stop losses, and letting the market take you out. Be proactive to size down if already proven otherwise.

In the end, the one who can manage losses effectively will ultimately emerge as the winner at the end of the tunnel.

'The best loser, is the long term winner'
