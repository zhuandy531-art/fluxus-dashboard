## Tweet Content

The latest relocation of the @tradingview screener to the right side involves integrating the V2 web screener (https://www.tradingview.com/screener/) directly with your existing TradingView platform. You can now directly click on the screened result for chart to be displayed on your platform. Extremely useful for live market screening (pre-market gainers, biggest live market mover etc)

In the meantime, this update does not affect the existing v1 screener but it allows us to concurrently use it for live market RVOL tracking.

Thank you @StarshipTrade for alerting!
