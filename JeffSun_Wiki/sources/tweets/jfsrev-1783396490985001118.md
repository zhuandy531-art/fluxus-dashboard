## Tweet Content

A trading strategy may offer positive +EV over time, but trading edge does not stem from the strategy alone. 

It also requires the skill in applying the strategy appropriately, within the optimal market conditions, along with solid risk control, and a qualitative approach to periodical adjustment of money management based on your own historical trades over a large sample size.

It's your ability, curiosity, and willingness to cultivate these skills that set you apart from others using the same strategy. That's why the real edge is unique and personal to each trader.

It is a distinct story for each of us.
