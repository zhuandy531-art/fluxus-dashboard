@BrianLeeTrades is one of the most transparent and insightful voices in this space when it comes to discussing risk management and the law of large numbers. His ideas is relevant to all strategies, even for longterm investors to explore. Despite his sizable following, I still find him extremely underrated given the depth of topics he covers. His content has personally influenced me—especially his “freezing” guide, which prompted me to adopt periodic account withdrawals and shift capital into yield-based assets (reason for my 2 additional income-generating properties I acquired in early 2025).

I always look forward to his periodic AMAs (he just posted one 2), and I highly recommend his interviews—particularly those with @traderlion and @InvestorsLive—as well as his Medium posts from 2018 to 2023.
