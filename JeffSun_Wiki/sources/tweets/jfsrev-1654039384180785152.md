## Tweet Content

In this market period, trading can be particularly difficult as it requires exercising restraint instead of constantly taking action.

I always carry a small notebook to jot down insightful quotes or ideas from books or tweets that can benefit my trading and thought processes. This notebook has become a sort of diary and reference material to track when specific influences occurred during the market's timeline.

One quote that I came across and would like to share is: "It is natural for humans to want to play every hand. The desire to always be in the game is one of the speculator's greatest enemies in managing his money." This quote is especially relevant given the current market headwinds and challenges. 

Let us truly contemplate and internalize the following quote: "Often, money that is just sitting can later be moved into the right situation at the right time."
