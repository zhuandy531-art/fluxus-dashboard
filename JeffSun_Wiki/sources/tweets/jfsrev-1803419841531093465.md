## Tweet Content

it took me about 5 years to breakeven, and i sincerely believe the biggest stride in trading proficiency is when a trader gets to breakeven period. here is why;

1) the basic foundation is strengthen, both technical, fundamental and, arguably the most underrated the share and capital structure of a stock
2) there's understanding of pre-market risk event, even events like 3rd friday option expiration is embedded in your head,
3) there is risk control, you become diligent with position sizing
4) there is keen interest to explore their own performance metrics from historical trades to improve further,
5) there is acceptance of losses as part of trading business.

all in all, there is elimination of 'unconscious incompetence', knowing what you don't know.

going forward even if it's a losing year, there is 'conscious incompetence' because you know what you did wrong in that year and it can be easily sorted or eliminated in entirety. I remember Qullamaggie had either 2022 or 2023 losing year because he didn't have to pay capital gain tax for that year, it doesn't make him a bad trader.
