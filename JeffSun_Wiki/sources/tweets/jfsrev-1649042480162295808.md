## Tweet Content

In this lackluster 3 full weeks of 'Death by a Thousand Cuts' pedal and brake market, it may be more advantageous to focus on frequently taking partial profits (or actively scaling out losses before stop) rather than pursuing a large profit all at once. 

Adopting the approach of "hitting lots of singles" can help to reduce the volatility of your account equity while still allowing you to remain engaged in the market, reducing profit loss frequency.

It's worth noting that this type of market behavior can challenge even the most experienced traders and serve as a reminder to stay humble.
