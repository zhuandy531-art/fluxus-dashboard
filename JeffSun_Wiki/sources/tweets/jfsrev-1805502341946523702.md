## Tweet Content

Food for Thought on Position Holding of Unrealized Losses

We all know that a longer average holding period for winning trades typically correlates with a higher risk-reward ratio in your monthly performance metrics. But have you considered applying the same analysis to the average holding period for losing trades?

If the rule of thumb is "the shorter, the better," would it not make sense to scale out of unprofitable trades before they hit your stop loss with your full position size? A quicker turnover unlocks available balance for potential opportunities to size in, but exploring this strategy would also positively impact your monthly profit factor directly. 

"If the market does not prove the position correct, it is still possible that the market has not proven the position wrong." - Phantom of the Pit
