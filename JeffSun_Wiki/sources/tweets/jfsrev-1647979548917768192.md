## Tweet Content

I just completed reading 'Phantom of the Pits', which I believe is a hidden gem. The author does not try to boast about Phantom's accomplishments but instead provides an honest account of the challenging world of trading for a living from his interview. The book does not focus on technical analysis, but rather offers valuable insights on managing losses, maintaining the right mindset, and handling money.

Interestingly, the book was not published for any commercial gain and was made available online for free in 1997. In my opinion, 'Phantom of the Pits' is one of the best books on trading that has not received the attention it deserves. I consider the 'Phantom' a legend, akin to the Market Wizards.

For anyone interested, the original e-book is 110 pages and widely available online for free download
