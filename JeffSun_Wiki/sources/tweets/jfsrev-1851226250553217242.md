Importance of Execution Quality

An A-rated setup can still result in C-rated execution if the entry quality, or exit aren’t managed effectively, leading to subpar outcomes despite a strong setup. Conversely, a C-rated setup—one with less ideal conditions—can yield A-rated execution if approached with precision, sizing, maximizing gains or minimizing losses in situations with limited potential. 

A great setup doesn’t guarantee great execution, just as a less-than-perfect setup can still be executed skillfully to produce solid results.
