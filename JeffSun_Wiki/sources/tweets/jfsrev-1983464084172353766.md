## Tweet Content

Low Beta is useful for diversifying correlation among your trading ideas before positioning to your portfolio.

But worth pointing out that a beta below absolute value of 1, or 100% (low correlation) can suggest 2 separate things:

i) High ADR% + Low Beta → independent volatility, less influenced by overall market moves (e.g. $BABA).

ii) Low ADR% + Low Beta → relatively stable stock; if the market moves 1%, it typically moves only a fraction of that. eg. $KO
