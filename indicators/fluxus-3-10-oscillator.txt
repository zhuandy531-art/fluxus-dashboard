Fluxus 3/10 Oscillator
======================

Linda Raschke's 3/10 Momentum Oscillator — a short-term momentum gauge
built from the spread between a fast and slow simple moving average.

Formula
-------
  Histogram  = SMA(close, 3) − SMA(close, 10)
  Signal     = SMA(histogram, 16)

How to Read
-----------
  • Histogram above zero → short-term trend is up
  • Histogram below zero → short-term trend is down
  • Histogram crosses above signal → momentum turning bullish
  • Histogram crosses below signal → momentum turning bearish
  • Divergence: price makes new low but oscillator makes higher low → bullish
  • "Slingshot": histogram pulls back to signal during a trend, then resumes
    → continuation entry

Settings
--------
  Fast MA Length    Default 3    (short-term average)
  Slow MA Length    Default 10   (medium-term average)
  Signal Length     Default 16   (signal smoothing)
  Source            Default close

  All colors are fully configurable:
  Histogram Rising, Histogram Falling, Signal Line, Zero Line.

Reference
